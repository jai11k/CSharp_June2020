You can take advantage of .NET framework on custom types by implementing the following
interfaces.
C# provides built-in interfaces that are useful to manage custom types for different useful
purposes like
defining custom collections and dispose resources that are no longer needed.

• IEnumerable & IEnemerable<T>
• IEnumerator & IEnumerator<T>
• ICollection & ICollection<T>
• IList & IList<T>
• IComparable & IComparable<T>
• IComparer & IComparer<T>
• IEquatable<T>
